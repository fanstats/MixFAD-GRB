Cluster Analysis and Variability Characterization of BATSE Catalog Gamma
Ray Bursts with Gaussian Mixture of Factor Analyzers
================

This project applies Gaussian mixtures of factor analyzers to cluster
and characterize gamma-ray bursts (GRBs) from the Burst and Transient
Source Experiment (BATSE) 4Br Catalog.

The density and scatter plots on the left show the nine astrophysical
features, while the distribution plots on the right highlight the
characteristics of the five estimated clusters in terms of three
composite features: duration ($T_{90}$), total fluence
($F_1 + F_2 + F_3 + F_4$), and spectral hardness
$\displaystyle F_3/(F_1 + F_2)$.
